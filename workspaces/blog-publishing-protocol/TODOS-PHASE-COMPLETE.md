## Key Decisions

### 1. Three-Pillar Architecture
- **Why**: Concentrates domain authority; creates topical hierarchy; enables exponential ranking growth
- **Pillars**: Custom Software, AI Integration, Digital Transformation
- **Clusters**: 12 articles per pillar (36 total) + 20 supporting articles = 56 total

### 2. 930 Words/Day Publishing Pace
- **Why**: Achieves 56 articles in 90 days; hits 100K visitor goal by day 120
- **Cadence**: 1 article every 2-3 days (Phase 1) → 2 articles/week (Phases 2-3)
- **Quality Gate**: 47-point SEO checklist prevents low-quality articles

### 3. Aggressive Backlink Strategy
- **Why**: Required for day-90 goal; without backlinks realistic timeline is day 120-150
- **Target**: 5-10 backlinks per pillar (15-30 total) by day 60
- **Methods**: Guest posts, founder mentions, community participation
