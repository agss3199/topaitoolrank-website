# ChatGPT Content Generation Prompts

## Overview

Use these prompts with ChatGPT to generate article drafts. Each prompt is formatted for copy-paste directly into ChatGPT. Customize the bracketed sections [LIKE THIS] for your specific article.

**Process:**
1. Copy the appropriate prompt template below
2. Replace all [BRACKETED] sections with your article details
3. Paste into ChatGPT
4. Generate article
5. Run through SEO optimization checklist before publishing

## PROMPT TEMPLATE 5: CASE STUDY (2,500-3,500 words)

```
You are an expert SEO writer. Write a detailed case study article for "[PRIMARY KEYWORD]".

   - Teaser: "Here's exactly how they did it"

2. H2: "The Challenge" (300-400 words)
   - What problem did [COMPANY] face?
   - Why was it urgent?
   - What were they trying to achieve?
   - Include 2-3 specific metrics showing the problem
   - Include customer/employee pain point quotes (if possible)

3. H2: "The Solution" (400-500 words)
   - What approach did they take?
   - Step-by-step: how they implemented the solution
   - Tools/technologies used

   - H3 sub-sections: Phase 1, Phase 2, Phase 3 (if applicable)

   - Resource requirements

7. Conclusion (100-150 words)
   - Recap: success is possible with [APPROACH]

SEO REQUIREMENTS:

- Keywords in 3-4 H2 headings
- Bolded key metrics and results

- Real company name and industry (or anonymized "[COMPANY NAME]")
- Specific, quantified results (not "improved significantly")

- 2-3 supporting metrics/statistics
- Visual: "INSERT: before/after screenshot" or "INSERT: timeline graphic"

- Specific challenges overcome (not generic ones)
- Replicable framework others can follow

- Intro: link to pillar page
- Challenge section: link to article about the problem
- Solution section: link to how-to articles
- Results: link to industry benchmark article

DO NOT INCLUDE:
- Unverified claims or exaggerated results
- Confidential/proprietary information
- Affiliate links to the company's product
- Company promotional content

Generate the complete 2,500-3,500-word case study following all specifications.
```

## How to Use These Prompts

### Step 1: Select Your Article Type
- Pillar page (4,500 words)
- How-to guide (2,000-2,500 words)
- Listicle (2,000-2,500 words)
- Comparison (2,500-3,000 words)
- Case study (2,500-3,500 words)

### Step 2: Customize the Template
Replace all [BRACKETED] sections:
- [ARTICLE TITLE] → Your specific title
- [PRIMARY KEYWORD] → Your target keyword
- [SECONDARY KEYWORDS] → Your list of 2-3 keywords
- [INDUSTRY] → Your niche/industry
- [H2 SECTIONS] → Your outline sections
- [INTERNAL LINK] → Your related articles

### Step 3: Copy-Paste into ChatGPT
- Go to chat.openai.com
- Create new chat
- Paste the complete filled-in prompt
- Click send

### Step 4: Review & Refine
- ChatGPT generates draft (~30 min reading time)
- Read through for quality and accuracy
- Request revisions: "Please expand the 'Cost Factors' section with more specific pricing examples"
- Download article

### Step 5: Run Through SEO Checklist
- Use `specs/seo-optimization-checklist.md` (47 points)
- Optimize title/meta/links
- Add images and alt text
- Publish

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: The more detailed your [BRACKETED] sections, the better the output
2. **Provide Context**: If customizing, explain your industry/audience in the prompt
3. **Request Revisions**: Ask ChatGPT to "Make this section more specific with examples" or "Reduce jargon for a general audience"
4. **Split Long Prompts**: If ChatGPT gets confused, break into 2 prompts (e.g., Intro + H2 sections in one prompt, Conclusion separately)
5. **Emphasize Metrics**: ChatGPT responds well to "Include specific numbers and percentages" - it will add more data
