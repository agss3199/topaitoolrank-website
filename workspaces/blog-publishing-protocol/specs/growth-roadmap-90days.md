## Scaling Beyond 90 Days

### If Behind on Traffic Projections

**Problem: Day 60 and only 500-1,000 organic visitors/month (should be 2K-8K)**

**Possible Causes:**
1. Articles not ranking (no impressions in GSC) → Internal linking weak; backlinks needed
2. High impressions, low clicks → Titles/meta descriptions not compelling
3. High bounce rate → Articles don't match search intent

**Solutions:**
- [ ] Build backlinks to pillar pages (guest posts, founder mentions, PR)
- [ ] Rewrite 3-5 lowest-CTR article titles with power words
- [ ] Review Google Search Console queries; update articles to match intent
- [ ] Add internal links from pillar pages to underperforming clusters

### If Ahead on Traffic Projections

**Problem: Day 60 and already 15,000+ organic visitors/month (ahead of schedule)**

**Opportunities:**
- Reduce publishing cadence (1 article/week instead of 2) → More time for quality
- Expand to new pillars/topics (start Pillar 4, 5, 6)
- Go deeper on high-performers (add advanced clusters)
- Begin building brand/authority (founder interviews, speaking, media mentions)

## Contingency Planning

**What if you miss publishing targets?**

- Skipped 1 article (1 week behind): Catch up by publishing 3 articles next week
- Skipped 1 week (7 days behind): Extend roadmap by 1 week; no penalty
- Skipped 2+ weeks (14+ days behind): Assess why (resource constraint? quality issue?); adjust plan

**What if you get low traffic despite publishing on schedule?**

- Likely cause: Poor internal linking or weak backlinks
- Action: Don't delay publishing. Focus on backlink building (guest posts, founder mentions)
- Pivot: Quality over quantity. 40 excellent articles > 60 okay articles
