# Blog Publishing Protocol — Growth to 100K Visitors in 3 Months

## User Intent

Create a structured protocol for publishing blog articles that ensures:
- Articles are interconnected (internal linking strategy)
- SEO-optimized (technical & on-page)
- Keyword-perfect (keyword research-driven)
- Scalable (repeatable template & flow)
- Outcome: Scale website to 1L (100,000) visitors in 3 months

## Scope

1. **Blog Publishing Protocol** — step-by-step process for creating/publishing articles
2. **Content Template** — article structure optimized for SEO
3. **Keyword & Topic Framework** — systematic approach to keyword research
4. **Interconnection Strategy** — internal linking, content clustering, pillar pages
5. **SEO Optimization Checklist** — on-page, technical, and UX signals
6. **Growth Roadmap** — content calendar strategy to reach 100K visitors in 90 days

## Key Questions

- What is the current website traffic baseline?
- What industry/niche is the website in?
- What's the primary traffic source (organic search, social, referral)?
- Does the website have existing content?
- What CMS/platform is used?
- Budget for tools (SEO, analytics, keyword research)?
