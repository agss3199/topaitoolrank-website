## Prompt 02: Word Counter Article

**Tool Name**: Word Counter
**Primary Keyword**: "word counter"
**Secondary Keywords**: "character count", "word count tool", "text counter"
**Target Audience**: Writers, students, content creators, marketers, translators

**Unique Angle**: Help readers understand how word/character count matters for different platforms (essays, social media, emails, billing).

**Hook**: "That email campaign text is 500 words? Or 150? You have no idea. Without a quick way to count, you're guessing."

**Key Use Cases**:

2. Content marketer checking blog post length for SEO (1500-2000 words optimal)
3. Translator billing by word count (need exact counts for invoices)
4. Social media manager checking character limits across platforms

**Tips Section**: Emphasize how word count affects Google SEO (200+ words minimal), email preview lengths, and platform-specific limits.

**FAQ**: Q: Do UTMs affect link quality or SEO? A: No, UTMs are just query parameters that help with analytics.
