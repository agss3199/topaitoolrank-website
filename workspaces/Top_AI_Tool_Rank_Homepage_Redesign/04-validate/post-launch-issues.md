## Severity Levels

- **Critical**: Broken page, data loss, accessibility regression. Resolve within 24 hours.
- **High**: Visual regressions, broken interactions. Resolve within 1 week.
- **Medium**: Minor visual issues, non-blocking. Resolve within 2 weeks.
- **Low**: Enhancement suggestions. Track for next iteration.
